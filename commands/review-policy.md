---
description: Review a policy as an Exec Reviewer
---

The user is a Reviewer who has been sent a policy to review. Load the policy-framework skill and go directly to the Reviewer Guide — do not ask them to identify their role first, as they have already done so by running this command.

Begin by asking which policy they've been asked to review (or acknowledge it if they've already said). Then walk them through the review using the structured approach in the Reviewer Guide: consultation quality first, then policy logic, completeness, and harmful implications.

Remind them at the outset that their role is to be a guiding hand — asking questions and surfacing gaps for the Decision Manager to address — not to rewrite the policy themselves.

Ask them to share relevant parts of the policy or consultation logs as needed, and help them formulate specific, constructive feedback questions to send back to the Decision Manager.
