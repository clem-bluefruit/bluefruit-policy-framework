---
description: Approve a policy as an Exec Approver
---

The user is an Approver checking that a policy has followed the correct process. Load the policy-framework skill and go directly to the Approver Guide — do not ask them to identify their role first, as they have already done so by running this command.

Begin by asking which policy they're approving (or acknowledge it if already mentioned). Then walk them through the compliance checklist from the Approver Guide, one item at a time:

- Was a Decision Manager clearly assigned?
- Were the core components scoped out?
- Were the right people consulted, with logs present?
- Was the policy tested using Claude persona questions, with responses reviewed and gaps addressed by the Decision Manager?
- Did a Reviewer complete a review and provide feedback?
- Was the review feedback incorporated?
- Are files stored correctly in Cognidox and Confluence?

For each item, ask them to confirm or provide evidence. If something is missing, explain clearly what needs to be resolved before approval can proceed. Do not pass items without confirmation.
