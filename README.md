# Bluefruit Policy Framework Plugin

This plugin guides you through Bluefruit's policy creation and approval process. Whether you're writing a new policy, reviewing one, or giving final approval, it walks you through your specific responsibilities step by step.

---

## How to use it

There are two ways to get started:

**Option 1 — Use a command** (recommended)

Type one of the following at the start of a conversation, depending on your role:

| Command | Who it's for |
|---|---|
| `/create-policy` | Decision Managers drafting or progressing a policy |
| `/review-policy` | Exec team members reviewing a policy |
| `/approve-policy` | Exec team members giving final process approval |

**Option 2 — Just describe what you're doing**

You don't have to use a command. If you start a conversation with something like "I'm working on the expenses policy" or "I've been asked to review a policy", the plugin will pick it up automatically and ask which role you're in.

---

## What each role involves

**Decision Manager** — You own the policy from start to finish. The plugin will guide you through scoping, consultations, drafting, testing with Claude persona questions, and seeing it through to approval and issuance.

**Reviewer** — You're an Exec team member checking the policy is sound. The plugin will remind you that your role is to ask questions and surface gaps — not to rewrite the policy yourself — and walk you through what to assess.

**Approver** — You're a different Exec team member checking that the correct process was followed. The plugin gives you a compliance checklist and works through it with you item by item.

---

## A note on consultations (Decision Managers)

The most common mistake in the process is turning up to a consultation with a finished draft and asking for sign-off. The plugin will flag this and redirect you if it looks like that's what you're about to do. Consultations are for understanding the problem — not for getting approval on your solution.

---

## Questions or feedback

If something doesn't feel right, or the guidance doesn't match your situation, let the plugin know in the conversation and it'll adapt. For changes to the process itself, speak to the Finance Director.
